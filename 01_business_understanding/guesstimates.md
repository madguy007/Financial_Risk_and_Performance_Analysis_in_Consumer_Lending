# 🧮 Guesstimates — FinTech Industry

## 📌 Objective

To estimate key FinTech trends using structured assumptions, logical reasoning, and step-by-step calculations.

---

## 1. 📈 Growth in Global FinTech Investments (Next 5 Years)

### Approach

Using **CAGR (Compound Annual Growth Rate)**

| Step | Assumption                        | Calculation                         |
| ---- | --------------------------------- | ----------------------------------- |
| 1    | Current Global FinTech Investment | Assume $100 Billion                 |
| 2    | Expected CAGR                     | 15%                                 |
| 3    | Growth Multiplier                 | 1 + 0.15 = 1.15                     |
| 4    | Value after 5 Years               | $100 × (1.15)^5 ≈ $201 Billion      |
| 5    | Percentage Increase               | (($201 - $100) / $100) × 100 = 101% |

### ✅ Final Answer

We estimate a **100%–105% increase**, effectively doubling the market size.
