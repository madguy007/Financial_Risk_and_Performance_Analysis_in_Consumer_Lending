# 🔍 Product Dissection — KreditBee

## 📌 Company Selection

**Company Chosen:** KreditBee

### Justification

* **Popularity:**
  KreditBee is one of India's leading digital loan lending platforms, with over **100 million app downloads** and **7 million+ active users**. Total credit disbursed exceeds **₹30,000 crore**.

* **Impact:**
  KreditBee disrupted traditional banking by introducing a **fully digital lending model**, leveraging alternative data (device data, location, SMS parsing) to serve users without traditional credit history. Loan disbursal time reduced from days → **under 10 minutes**.

* **Relevance:**
  Targets the “**missing middle**” — young professionals and middle-class users lacking collateral or credit history. Offers unsecured loans from ₹6,000 to ₹10 lakhs, improving financial inclusion.

---

## ⚙️ Core Features & Functionalities

### 1. Tiered Personal Loans (Flexi & Salaried)

* Offers small-ticket to large-ticket loans
* Builds user credit lifecycle

**Impact:**
Improves **Customer Lifetime Value (LTV)** via upselling

---

### 2. 100% Digital KYC & Automated Underwriting

* OCR-based document verification
* API integration with credit bureaus

**Impact:**

* Reduces **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)**
* Enables instant approvals

---

### 3. KreditBee UPI Integration

* Users can link bank accounts via UPI

**Impact:**

* Increases **Daily Active Users (DAU)**
* Converts app into daily utility

---

### 4. Seamless Repayment Ecosystem

* Integrated payment gateways
* Auto-debit EMIs

**Impact:**

* Improves **collection efficiency (94–97%)**
* Reduces repayment friction

---

## 🌍 Real-World Problems & Solutions

### Problem 1: New-to-Credit (NTC) Users Excluded

* Traditional banks reject users without credit history

**Solution:**

* AI-based underwriting using alternative data

---

### Problem 2: Slow Loan Processing

* Traditional loans take days

**Solution:**

* Fully digital process → approval in minutes

---

### Problem 3: Dependence on Informal Lenders

* Loan sharks charge 100–200% interest

**Solution:**

* Transparent, RBI-compliant lending
* Regulated APR (17%–50%)
